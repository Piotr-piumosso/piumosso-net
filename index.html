Inside the guide

What you'll learn

01

Programme Selection

How to choose repertoire that fits your event type, guest profile, and venue atmosphere — without needing musical expertise.

02

Acoustic Setup

The ten-point checklist that determines whether live music enhances or disrupts your event. Venue shape, noise floor, room reverb.

03

Timing & Flow

When to start, when to pause, how to integrate music into dinner service or speeches without breaking momentum.

04

Briefing the Artist

The five questions that prevent 80% of day-of surprises. What professional ensembles need from you — and what they should be providing.

05

Budget Breakdown

Where the fee actually goes. Travel, preparation, equipment, programme notes — understanding the real cost of live music.

06

Red Flags

Warning signs when vetting ensembles: generic repertoire lists, no recorded samples, unclear logistics, vague pricing.

Built from experience,
not from theory

Più Mosso is a violin & piano duo (Zofia Andersz and Piotr Górski) based in Poland, performing across Central Europe. The guide is drawn from our own event work — what we've seen go wrong, what guests remember, and what event directors never think to ask.

We created this resource because most event planners we speak with have had at least one live music experience that fell flat — usually for reasons that were entirely preventable.
